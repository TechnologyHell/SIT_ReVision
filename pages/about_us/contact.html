Location & Accessibility

SIT is strategically located in Sukna, Siliguri, at the foothills of the Himalayas, offering a serene environment conducive to learning and innovation. The institute is well-connected by road, rail, and air.

By Road: Easily accessible via NH-31, with frequent public and private transportation options.

By Rail: The nearest railway station, New Jalpaiguri (NJP), is approximately 15 km away, providing connectivity to major cities across India.

By Air: The nearest airport, Bagdogra International Airport (IXB), is around 20 km from the campus, offering both domestic and international flights.
