Guiding the Institution Towards Excellence

Siliguri Institute of Technology (SIT) is led by a team of visionary leaders dedicated to academic excellence, institutional growth, and student success. The leadership team comprises experienced professionals and academicians who bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the institute.

Board of Governors

The Board of Governors oversees the strategic development of SIT, ensuring that the institute adheres to its mission and vision. The board consists of prominent academicians, industry experts, and policy-makers committed to shaping the future of education at SIT.

“At SIT, we believe in nurturing minds, fostering innovation, and empowering our students to excel in their chosen fields. Our commitment to academic excellence, research, and industry collaboration ensures that we create professionals who can contribute meaningfully to society.”

Dr. Mithun Chakraborty

Principal, Siliguri Institute of Technology

Academic Council

The Academic Council is responsible for curriculum development, academic policies, and maintaining high educational standards. It includes department heads, senior faculty members, and external academic advisors who ensure that SIT’s programs remain relevant and industry-oriented.

Department Heads

Each academic department at SIT is managed by a dedicated Head of Department (HOD) who ensures smooth academic operations, faculty development, and student engagement. They play a crucial role in mentoring students and enhancing research initiatives within their respective disciplines.

Administrative Leadership

SIT's administrative team ensures the efficient management of resources, student services, and institutional infrastructure. This includes:

1

Registrar

Overseeing institutional administration and compliance.

2

Controller of Examinations

Managing academic assessments and results.

3

Training and Placement Officers

Bridging the gap between students and industry for better career opportunities.

4

Student Welfare Officer

Addressing student concerns and fostering a supportive campus environment.
