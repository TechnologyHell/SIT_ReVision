Established in 1999 under the Techno India Group, Siliguri Institute of Technology (SIT) is the largest private educational institution in North Bengal. Located near Siliguri city, the campus is surrounded by lush greenery, tea gardens, and the Sub-Himalayan ranges, providing an ideal environment for learning and research. With a strong emphasis on academic excellence and holistic development, SIT nurtures future professionals through innovative teaching methodologies and industry collaborations.

Vision and Mission

SIT is committed to academic excellence, aiming to produce highly skilled professionals who are industry-ready and globally employable. The institute focuses on innovative learning methodologies, hands-on training, and research-driven education to create leaders in engineering, management, and technology.

Research & Development

The institution hosts a dedicated Research and Development (R&D) Cell and is a recognized Remote Centre of IIT Bombay. It regularly conducts national-level workshops and training programs under the National Mission on Education through ICT, funded by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India. The research initiatives encourage students and faculty to engage in cutting-edge technology development and industry collaborations.

Location Advantage

Siliguri, the commercial capital of North Bengal, is strategically located as a gateway to the northeastern states of India. The region offers a unique blend of urban convenience and natural beauty, with close proximity to major tourist destinations like Darjeeling, Sikkim, and Bhutan. This geographical advantage makes SIT an attractive destination for students across the country.
